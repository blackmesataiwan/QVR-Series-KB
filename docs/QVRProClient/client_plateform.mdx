### QVR Pro Client

QVR Pro Client is a cross-platform client utility that allows users to connect to QVR Pro, QVR Elite, QVR Center, or QVR Guard servers to monitor surveillance videos. You can monitor live streams and play back recorded videos at the same time on a highly-customizable interface.

- **Support : Windows/Mac OS**

:::info

If you want to watch H.265 (HEVC) format video streaming in Windows, you need to purchase HEVC licenses.

:::

### QVR Smart Client

QVR Smart Client allows you to directly view surveillance footage by connecting the NAS with an HDMI output to an HDMI display.<br/>
QVR Smart Client needs a license to use. You could buy it from https://software.qnap.com/qvr-ai-pack.html

- **Support : QNAP NAS HD Station**

### QVR Pro Client for Mobile

QVR Pro Client for Mobile provides an intuitive interface for your QVR Pro/Elite server, helping you manage IP cameras, monitor live views, and play back previous recordings using your mobile devices.

- **Support : iOS/iPadOS/Android**

### QVR Viewer

QVR Viewer allows you to watch your QVR Pro, QVR Elite, and QVP series NVR cameras. You could easily check the camera views on your big screen.

- **Support : Apple TV**
